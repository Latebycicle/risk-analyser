# Sambhav Foundation - Operational Context

## Zero-Cost Activities (Do NOT Flag)
The following activities are administrative and do NOT require a specific budget line:
- **Approvals:** Taking approval from donors, internal stakeholders, or partners.
- **Communication:** Sending emails, scheduling meetings, or coordination calls.
- **Reporting:** Routine submission of reports (unless specific "Impact Assessment" tools are required).
- **Signing:** Signing agreements, MoUs, or contracts.

## Budget Mappings
- **HR/Hiring:** If "Human Resource" costs are missing, check for "Salary" or "Project Management Fees".
- **Mobilization:** Can be covered under "Operational Cost" or "Marketing".
